Martin Fink

Founder · Engineer · Open-source long-timer

Ferrite Engineering was founded by Martin Fink, an engineer who has spent his career at the intersection of open source, hardware, and the systems that connect the two. He started Ferrite for one reason: the open-source community has built an extraordinary set of EDA tools, and there's a clear gap between those tools and the polished, integrated products engineering teams actually want to use day-to-day. Closing that gap — without abandoning the open-source foundations that make it possible — is what Ferrite is here to do.
